Chopsuey

Chop Suey is a stir-fry dish popular in Chinese cuisine, featuring a mix of vegetables like bean sprouts, celery, and bell peppers, often combined with protein such as pork, chicken, or shrimp. It's cooked with a savory sauce made from soy sauce and sometimes oyster sauce, resulting in a flavorful and colorful dish that is typically served with rice or noodles. üçõ

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • black pepper
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 4 hotdogs, sliced

Steps

  1. Slice the hotdogs, mince the garlic, and chop the onion and bell pepper.
  2. In a pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
  3. Add garlic and onion to the pan, saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Toss in the sliced hotdogs and bell pepper, stir-fry for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in soy sauce and oyster sauce, mix well and cook for a few more minutes until everything is well-coated and heated through.
