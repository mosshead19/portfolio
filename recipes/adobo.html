Simple Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish known for its savory, tangy flavor. It's typically made by marinating meat (usually chicken or pork) in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper, then simmering until tender. It's often served with rice and is a beloved staple in Filipino cuisine. üçõ

Ingredients

  • onion
  • pepper
  • garlic
  • soy sauce
  • 3 cups of water
  • msg
  • 1kg pork

Steps

  1. Mix chopped pork with onion, garlic, peppers, soy sauce, 2 cups of water, and MSG.
  2. Heat the pan and cook the mix until almost dry
  3. Add water as needed to soften the pork
